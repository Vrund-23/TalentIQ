Please create a file named .env.local in the root of your project and add the following:

MONGODB_URI=mongodb+srv://<username>:<password>@<cluster>.mongodb.net/codeArena
JWT_SECRET=your_super_secret_jwt_key_here

# Email Configuration (Nodemailer)
EMAIL_USER=your_email@gmail.com
EMAIL_PASS=your_app_specific_password_here

Note: 
1. Replace <username>, <password>, and <cluster> with your actual MongoDB Atlas credentials.
2. For Gmail, you MUST use an "App Password" (generate in Google Account > Security > 2-Step Verification > App passwords), not your regular password.
